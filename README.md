# Repair Café of the Peninsula

This is the website for **Repair Café of the Peninsula (RCP)**, a free, grassroots community repair event coming to the San Francisco Bay Peninsula in late summer 2026.

Visitors bring broken items and work hands-on alongside skilled volunteer repair coaches to fix them together.

The site covers what RCP is, the repair disciplines we support, volunteer roles, tool donation needs, and a sign-up form for anyone who wants to get involved or stay in the loop.

---

For more information about the Repair Café movement, visit the [Repair Café International Foundation](https://www.repaircafe.org/en).
